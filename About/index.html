Welcome to KBP Polytechnic, Satara

Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil Polytechnic, Satara, is a renowned institution affiliated to MSBTE Mumbai and approved by AICTE Delhi and DTE Maharashtra State. Founded with a vision to provide quality education to all sections of society, our institution has been a beacon of knowledge and empowerment.

Since its inception, KBP Polytechnic has been dedicated to the education of the underprivileged, emphasizing the importance of education in tackling social issues such as casteism, illiteracy, and economic inequality. With a network of branches across Maharashtra and Karnataka, we serve thousands of students from diverse backgrounds.

Our mission is to nurture talent, foster creativity, and prepare students to become competent professionals in their respective fields. We strive to provide a holistic educational experience that equips students with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in the ever-evolving global landscape.

We firmly believe in the motto "Education through self-help is the best education," and it drives our approach to teaching and learning.

Our Goal & Journey

Since our inception, our goal has been to empower individuals through education, contributing to the development of society. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, innovation, and dedication to academic excellence.
